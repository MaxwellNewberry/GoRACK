About Us

GoRACK, established in 2009

1st Data Center Opened
January 2009
GoRACK opened our first data center at 800 Water St, Jacksonville, Florida. We started with two racks, two clients and 10,000 square feet of floor space. We’ve come a long way since the beginning.
SSAE 16 SOC 1 Type II Certification
June 2012
In 2012 GoRACK archived the SSAE 16 SOC 1 Type II certified data center.
2nd Data Center Opened
August 2015
GoRACK opened our second data center at 421 W. Church St, Jacksonville, Florida. This location is a culmination of everything we learned from the previous 5 years with our first location.
GoRACK Files Trademark
October 2016
In 2016 GoRACK filed for a Trademark on our name and design. This was one of the many great steps in solidifying our business.
GoRACK Cloud Development Begins
December 2016
GoRACK began the planning of their Cloud Service in early 2016. In December of 2016 our GoRACK Cloud became readily available for beta testing. This expansion provides a simple and affordable solution to any business.
GoRACK Cloud Launched
April 2017
GoRACK Cloud Solution went live in April of 2017 to thunderous applause heard all around the office. Ask your IT department if the GoRACK Cloud Solution is right for you.
