Frequent Questions
What is GoRACK Cloud?

GoRACK Cloud is as simple as a bucket of tools to build your IT environment. Instead of having the hassle of buying and configuring expensive server equipment, you can purchase a set of resources and allocate them to create as many servers you need in more than one location.

That sounds nice, but is it expensive?

Actually cloud hosting is an affordable solution compared to most hosting options. You are billed by the hour based on the amount of resources you need. This helps you save money by only billing you for the resources you use versus paying for allocated resources you don't use.

This sounds complicated, what if I'm not "server savvy?"

We have a user-friendly interface designed for ease of use to help you create your virtual machines. After you order, login credentials are provided for the GoRACK Cloud Panel. If you have any issues, we also have a 24/7 support team.

Sounds great! How do I order?

Contact our sales department! If you click here, or scroll down on this page you will be prompted with a contact form. There you will describe the resources you need and one of our sales representatives will assist you.

Automated Backups & Failovers

Schedule your servers for automatic backups or manually take one at any time. You can even convert your backups into a template and spin up other virtual machines. Just like your ISOs, you can make your templates public to other GoRACK Cloud clients.

Custom ISOs

If your software requires a custom version of Linux or another Operating System, you can upload your own ISO file. This allows our cloud users to have access to all operating systems.

Self Healing Cloud

Our self healing capabilities removes the possibility of hardware failures interupting your service. This prevents bad sectors from effecting your data.

Instant Scaling

Automatically add or reduce RAM and CPU based on parameters you configure. The ability to scale to what you need not only saves you time, but saves you big money. Don't let limitations on storage hold you back. Add more HDD or SSD Storage to fit your needs.

Global Federation

We have a federated partnership with over 50 data centers from around the world. Spin up VMs closer to your customers to reduce latency.
